Who we
are

A complete, end-to-end agentic AI use case — one autonomous pipeline that plans, crawls, retrieves, reasons and self-verifies, with no human in the loop.

FIE reads a company's public website the way a first-time visitor would — then reports back what actually lands, what confuses, and what's missing. It doesn't guess at intent or invent facts: every observation is pulled from a real page and cited, and if the evidence is too thin to be fair, FIE says so rather than filling the gap. One grounded report per company — the same rigor a careful human reviewer would apply, run end to end by a multi-model pipeline.
